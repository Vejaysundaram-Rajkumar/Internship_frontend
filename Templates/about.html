About Subtitle Genie

Welcome to Subtitle Genie, your ultimate solution for hassle-free subtitle generation! Our application is designed to simplify the process of creating subtitles for your video content. Whether you're an educator creating training videos, a content creator making engaging videos, or a professional needing accurate transcriptions, Subtitle Genie is here to help.

How It Works

  1. Upload Your Video: Start by uploading your video file in one of the supported formats (mp4, avi, mov).
  2. Automatic Processing: Once uploaded, our application automatically extracts the audio from the video and processes it to generate subtitles.
  3. Download Your Subtitles: After processing, you can easily download the generated subtitle file (.srt format) to your desired location.

  • Efficient and Fast: Get your subtitles ready in just a few minutes, saving you time and effort.
